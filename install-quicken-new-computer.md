# Installing Quicken on a New Computer Without a CD Drive

If you are setting up a new computer and need to install Quicken but your system does not have a CD/DVD drive, you can still complete the installation digitally.

This guide explains how to install Quicken on a new computer using your Quicken account.

**Button Text:** Get Started With Quicken

---

## Things to Prepare Before Installation

Before installing Quicken, make sure you have:

- Internet connection
- Quicken ID and password
- Backup of your old Quicken data file
- Access to your purchase email

---

## Step 1: Sign In to Your Quicken Account

1. Open a browser on your new computer
2. Go to the official Quicken website
3. Sign in using the email address used for purchase

This allows you to download Quicken without using a CD.

---

## Step 2: Download Quicken

After signing in:

- Open your account dashboard
- Locate your Quicken product
- Click on Download

Save the installer file to your computer.

---

## Step 3: Install Quicken Software

1. Double-click the downloaded file
2. Follow the on-screen instructions
3. Sign in with your Quicken ID when prompted

Your license will automatically activate.

---

## Step 4: Restore Your Data Backup

1. Open Quicken
2. Select Restore from Backup
3. Choose your saved data file

Your accounts and history will be restored.

---

## Common Problems During Installation

- Installer does not open
- Incorrect login credentials
- Membership expired message

Most of these issues can be resolved by signing in again or restarting the installation.

---

## Final Notes

Installing Quicken on a new computer without a CD drive is simple and secure when done through your account.
Always keep backups before switching systems.
