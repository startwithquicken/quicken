# How to Fix Quicken Login Issues

Login issues can prevent access to Quicken features and data.

This guide helps you resolve common sign-in problems.

---

## Common Login Problems

- Incorrect email or password
- Account locked
- Login page not loading

---

## How to Fix Login Issues

### Reset Password
Use the password recovery option on the login screen.

### Check Internet Connection
Ensure stable connectivity.

### Update Quicken
Older versions may not support login properly.

---

## If Login Still Fails

- Restart your computer
- Try signing in via browser
- Verify your account email

---

## Conclusion

Most Quicken login problems are temporary and can be fixed by resetting credentials or updating the software.
